Professional Book Binding & Print Finishing in Norwich

The perfect finish for every project. From robust PUR bound paperbacks to high-quality square-back zines and portfolios, we provide complete, in-house finishing to ensure your publication feels exactly as good as it looks.

In-House Finishing You Can Trust

A beautifully printed page is only half the journey. The way your project is bound dictates its durability, its presentation, and how it is experienced by your readers. Because we handle all our print finishing directly in our Thorpe St Andrew facility, we maintain complete quality control from the moment the ink hits the raw 320x450mm or 450x640mm paper sheets to the final trim. No outsourcing, no delays—just expert craftsmanship.

1. Square Back Binding (Folded & Stapled)

Get the premium, flat-spine look of a perfect-bound book with the cost-effectiveness of traditional saddle stitching. Square back binding presses the folded spine flat, eliminating the "spring" of a standard stapled booklet.

Ideal for: Independent zine creators, limited edition 500-unit merchandise runs, local history booklets, and heavily illustrated publications focusing on atmospheric art, folklore, or niche subjects where aesthetic presentation is everything.
[3] Thick, professional paperback book angled to show the smooth, solid glue line at the spine.

2. PUR Perfect Binding

Polyurethane Reactive (PUR) binding is the gold standard for paperback books. PUR glue is significantly stronger and more flexible than traditional EVA glue. It won't crack when the book is opened flat, and it withstands extreme temperatures, making it incredibly durable regardless of the paper stock used.

Ideal for: Novels, thick corporate catalogues, high-end graphic novels, and annual reports.
[4] Sleek, wire-bound document with a clear acetate cover and heavy cardstock back, opened flat.

3. Wire Binding

When your document needs to lay completely flat or fold back on itself 360 degrees, wire binding (or wire-o binding) is the most practical and professional choice. We offer a range of wire colours and capacities to suit the thickness of your document.

Ideal for: Technical manuals, custom sketchbooks, training documents, and presentation decks.

Beyond the Bind: Trimming, Creasing & Custom Finishing

A truly bespoke print run often requires a few extra touches. We don't just bind; we ensure every detail of your finishing is immaculate.

Precision Trimming

We cut down custom sheet sizes to your exact specifications, ensuring edge-to-edge bleed without a millimeter of error.

Creasing & Scoring

Essential for heavier paper stocks (like 300gsm+ covers) to prevent the ink or paper fibers from cracking along the fold, keeping dark, rich colours and illustrations pristine.

Lamination

Choose from matte, gloss, or soft-touch laminates to protect your covers and add a premium tactile feel.
