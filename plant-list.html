Trade Print Capabilities & Plant List in Norwich

For over four decades, GoWise has been the trusted production partner for businesses, design agencies, and independent publishers across Norfolk and the UK.

By keeping our core printing and finishing processes entirely in-house, we offer trade buyers complete control over quality, turnaround times, and technical specifications.

Digital Press Capabilities

Our digital suite is built for speed, variable data, and colour reproduction that standard CMYK digital presses simply cannot achieve.

  • Fujifilm 5UPER COLOUR© Digital Press: Our flagship 5-colour system hits specific RGB tones and simulates Pantone® colours with exceptional accuracy.
  • Variable Data Printing (VDP): Full capability to swap text, numbering, and imagery sheet-to-sheet.
  • Short-Run Efficiency: Cost-effective for runs up to 500 units and bespoke merchandise.
  • Custom Sheet Handling: Configured for custom raw sizes like 320x450mm.

Lithographic Press Capabilities

For long runs and exact brand colour matching, traditional litho remains unbeatable.

  • Four-Colour Process (CMYK) Litho: High-accuracy production for catalogues, brochures, and commercial flyers.
  • Spot Colour Pantone® Matching: Exact ink mixing before the press runs for absolute colour fidelity.
  • Large Format Sheet Yields: Capacity to process larger raw stock sizes, such as 450x640mm.

In-House Finishing & Book Binding

We do not outsource our binding. Our fully equipped finishing department operates as a dedicated trade finishing service.

  • PUR Perfect Binding: Polyurethane Reactive adhesive for superior page pull strength and lay-flat flexibility.
  • Square Back Binding: Automated folding and stapling that presses the spine flat, mimicking a perfect-bound look.
  • Wire-O Binding: Precision punching for manuals requiring a 360-degree fold.
  • Pre-Press & Artwork: Full in-house department to inspect bleeds, colour profiles, and imposition layouts.

Partner With GoWise

We work seamlessly with trade buyers, offering white-label shipping and competitive trade pricing on both long and short runs.
