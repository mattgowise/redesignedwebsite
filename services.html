Complete Print, Binding & Design Services in Norwich

Finding a print partner who understands the nuances of your project shouldn't be difficult. Whether you need a massive corporate brochure rollout or a highly specialized, 500-unit run of an independent zine, GoWise delivers uncompromising quality from our Thorpe St Andrew facility.

By housing advanced printing technology, comprehensive book binding, and an expert pre-press design team under one roof, we ensure your project transitions seamlessly from a digital file to a perfectly finished physical product. Explore our core services below to see how we bring your ideas to the page.

1. Commercial & Independent Printing

We don't believe in a one-size-fits-all approach to ink and paper. We match your specific run size, budget, and colour requirements with the ideal printing press, ensuring flawless detail and maximum cost-efficiency.

  • Spot Colour Lithographic Printing When brand consistency is paramount, our traditional litho presses deliver. We are specialists in exact Pantone® ink matching, making this the perfect choice for corporate stationery, packaging, and high-volume runs where extreme precision and vibrant solid colours are non-negotiable.
  • 5-Colour Digital Printing Our cutting-edge Fujifilm 5UPER COLOUR© press goes far beyond standard CMYK. By utilizing an expanded colour gamut, we achieve incredibly rich RGB tones and simulate Pantone colours with stunning accuracy. Digital is fast, cost-effective for shorter runs, and perfect for variable data printing.
  • Custom Formats & Stocks We optimize your artwork for both standard and custom raw sheet sizes (including 320x450mm and 450x640mm) to reduce waste and keep your costs down, whether you're printing heavy-stock art prints or high-volume flyers.

2. Book Binding & In-House Finishing

A beautifully printed page is only half the journey; the finish dictates how your publication is experienced. Because we handle all our binding in-house, we maintain complete quality control without the delays of outsourcing.

  • PUR Perfect Binding The gold standard for paperback books, thick catalogues, and graphic novels. Polyurethane Reactive (PUR) glue is significantly stronger and more flexible than standard adhesives, ensuring your book won't crack even when opened completely flat.
  • Square Back Binding Get the premium, flat-spine look of a perfect-bound book with the cost-effectiveness of stapling. Ideal for independent creators, atmospheric folklore zines, and limited-edition lookbooks, this method presses the folded spine flat so your publication stacks and presents beautifully.
  • Wire Binding When your manual, sketchbook, or presentation deck needs to lay entirely flat or fold back 360 degrees, our durable wire-o binding offers the most practical and professional finish.

Digital vs. Lithographic Printing

Which is right for you? Choosing the right press depends entirely on your quantity, timeline, and quality requirements.

Digital Printing

Best for short to medium runs (typically 1 to 500 units).

Because there are no traditional printing plates to set up, digital is incredibly fast and cost-effective for smaller batches, limited-edition art prints, and variable data projects.

Lithographic Printing (Litho)

Best for high-volume commercial runs.

Litho uses wet ink and custom plates. The initial setup takes longer and costs more, but once the press is running, the cost-per-page drops drastically. Litho also allows us to use traditional Pantone® spot colours and print on much larger raw sheet sizes (such as 450x640mm) for maximum yield.
